About S.E Hinton

---------------------------------------------------

when she was growing up, she was sort of a tomboy. Which means she was a girl who liked "boy" things.

She had also been writing stories after she learned how to read. She said she loved making up her own stories

When her friend got beaten up on the street, she wrote a story about her friend getting beaten up and it turned out as the bestselling book The Outsiders

Who would think that some random sixteen year old could write a bestselling book

Fun fact the book sold over 15 million copies
